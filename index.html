    About Me

    Welcome to my portfolio! I am an 8th-semester Computer Engineering student with a strong focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and AI-driven applications. My Final Year Project, OptiPros, is a blind assistance device designed to help visually impaired individuals navigate their surroundings. It runs a Large Language Model (LLM) locally on a constrained Raspberry Pi 4, ensuring real-time processing without relying on cloud services.


    Beyond academics, I am passionate about Agentic AI and developing autonomous AI systems that adapt to real-world scenarios. I am also exploring AWS Cloud for deploying scalable AI solutions. Additionally, I work on Flutter development, creating AI-powered mobile applications that integrate machine learning models for intelligent user experiences. My portfolio highlights my expertise in NLP, embedded AI, cloud computing, and AI app development.
