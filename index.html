    Welcome to my portfolio! I am an 8th-semester Computer Engineering student with a strong focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and AI-driven applications. My Final Year Project, OptiPros, is a blind assistance device designed to help visually impaired individuals navigate their surroundings.
